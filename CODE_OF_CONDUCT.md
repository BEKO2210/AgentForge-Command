# Code of Conduct

This project follows the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
That document is the canonical text — please read it if you intend to take part in
issues, pull requests, or discussions on this repository.

In short:

- Be welcoming, respectful, and helpful.
- Disagreements happen — keep them about the work, not the person.
- Personal attacks, threats, and discriminatory behaviour are not tolerated.
- Don't share other people's private information without their permission.

If something falls short of that, report it through the private channel listed in
[`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md). The maintainer will look into it confidentially and act
fairly.
